francie cohen

Francie Cohen, a 20-year-old artist and culture enthusiast from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, showcases immense promise in her creative journey. With a love for vintage fashion and eclectic styles, she explores vibrant and abstract art through digital and mixed mediums. Francie's work reflects themes of community, relationships, and love, and she aspires to create spaces for women to share their stories.
