about

Francie Cohen, a 20-year-old visionary artist hailing from the vibrant city of Milwaukee, Wisconsin. With her retro-inspired style and a penchant for vintage fashion, Francie captivates audiences with her colorful and playful artwork. She ventures into the world of mixed media, employing digital art, crayon on canvas, and even painting to bring her bold and abstract creations to life. Through her expressive pieces, Francie delves into the realms of community, relationships, and love, delivering a unique artistic perspective that leaves viewers in awe.

While Francie's journey as an artist may have started with simple doodles, her passion for creativity has evolved into an exploration of the intersections between culture, media, and art. As she pursues her studies in culture and media at the New School, Francie channels her passion for storytelling into creating spaces for women from all walks of life to share their narratives. Her dedication to amplifying the voices of underrepresented communities is exemplified by her work on a documentary that highlighted the vibrant community groups of the Lower East Side and a written piece focusing on women in comedy.

Always eager to expand her horizons, Francie envisions a future where she merges her artistic talents and media expertise. Her ultimate goal is to build an app or platform geared towards young women, providing a space for them to share their experiences while raising awareness about pressing global and personal issues. Francie's art reflects her profound appreciation for the world around her.

With a dynamic mix of fashion, sustainability, and representation, Francie Cohen emerges as a passionate multi-hyphenate, continuously seeking unique and engaging opportunities to strengthen her abilities and make a lasting impact in her artistic pursuits. For a glimpse into her vibrant world, you can find her sharing her work on Instagram under the handle @franciecohen.
